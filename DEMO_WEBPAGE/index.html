BMCC Student Project • CS Major Advising Assistant

Smarter Academic Support for CS Department Students

The BMCC CS Advising App is an on-demand chatbot designed for Computer Information Systems, Computer Network Technology, Computer Science, Geographic Information Science, and Cybersecurity students. It combines curriculum guidance, degree progress support, career pathways, alumni insight, transfer options, and internship or research information in one clear platform.

Core Features

Our chatbot combines structured academic support with career and opportunity guidance so students can make better decisions with less confusion.

01

Advising Support

Helps students understand degree requirements, next-course options, and planning pathways such as a 2-year or 3-year pace.

02

Career Pathways

Provides major-based career pathways so students can connect their interests to practical next steps.

03

Alumni Insight

Highlights BMCC alumni experiences across supported majors so students can see practical outcomes and stay motivated.

04

Transfer & Opportunities

Brings together transfer information, internships, and research opportunities into one accessible support system by major.

Menu Branching Structure

The chatbot is organized with branching options so students can move from a main category into more detailed support instead of receiving only one fixed response.

Main Menu

  • User Mode (read-only)
  • Admin Mode (password-protected)
  • Advising
  • Career Pathways
  • Transfer Options
  • Internship / Research

Submenu Examples

  • Select major/program
  • View degree requirements
  • Explore alumni advice
  • Compare transfer schools
  • Find internship or research opportunities
  • Admin add/delete data in files

How the System Works

The project uses C++ classes, vectors, and file handling to organize student information and respond with targeted answers.

1

Collect Information

Course data, advising information, alumni insight, transfer resources, and internship or research details are gathered from BMCC-related sources.

2

Organize Data

The information is structured using separate C++ classes and container-based logic to keep each feature clear and manageable.

3

Track Student Progress

Completed courses can be saved and used to filter curriculum guidance, helping students see what they already finished and what comes next.

4

Generate Helpful Responses

The chatbot provides filtered, concise, and relevant support so students can understand their options more quickly and confidently.

Project Impact

This solution aims to reduce confusion, improve planning, and make advising more accessible for students who may not always be able to get immediate help.

Clearer Planning

Students can understand degree progress and next steps without feeling lost in complicated degree maps or scattered information.

Better Decisions

Career guidance, alumni motivation, and transfer resources help students make more informed academic and professional choices.

Time and Cost Savings

By helping students avoid unnecessary classes or poor course sequencing, the app can support smarter progress toward graduation.

Future Vision

We want to keep improving this project by creating a stronger user interface, expanding it into a web and mobile-friendly platform, and eventually integrating an LLM for more dynamic, personalized, and human-like interaction.

The app now supports multiple CS-related majors with role-based access (Admin/User). The next phase is improving UI/UX and adding more automation and personalization.
