What the App Can Do

Our project brings together academic advising, degree planning, career exploration, and real-world opportunities into a student-friendly platform.

Academic Advising

Helps students understand course pathways, degree requirements, and what classes to take next.

Career Guidance

Shows GIS-related career paths, alumni experiences, and motivation from real professionals.

Transfer & Opportunities

Brings together transfer options, internship information, and research opportunities in one place.

Why This Project Matters

Advising offices are often busy, degree maps can feel confusing, and students may be unsure about their next steps. This app aims to make support clearer, faster, and easier to access.

Less Confusion

Students can quickly find the information they need without feeling overwhelmed by scattered resources.

Better Planning

Progress tracking and advising support help students avoid taking the wrong classes and wasting time or money.

More Confidence

Career guidance, alumni insight, and transfer pathways can help students make stronger academic decisions.

The Future Vision

We hope to turn this into a polished web and mobile-friendly experience, with more dynamic conversation features, stronger personalization, and future LLM integration. In the long term, this project could expand beyond GIS to support CS, CIS, and eventually all BMCC students across different majors.
